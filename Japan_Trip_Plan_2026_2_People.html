Day-by-Day Plan

Readable cards, no text lost on photos. Images are separate, details stay on solid panels.

Day 1-2
Sep 16-17Tokyo

Fly Calgary → Tokyo

Arrive at Narita, grab Welcome Suica, check in, light night walk in Shinjuku or Shibuya.

Keep this day easy. Main goal is landing, food, sleep, and getting oriented.

Day 3
Sep 18Anime

Akihabara + Asakusa

Anime stores, arcades, gachapon, Pokémon Center, then Senso-ji temple in the evening.

Good first full day because everything is easy by train.

Day 4
Sep 19Tokyo

Harajuku, Meiji Shrine, Shibuya

Meiji Shrine, Takeshita Street, Shibuya Crossing, Shibuya Sky around sunset.

Book Shibuya Sky ahead if you want sunset.

Day 5
Sep 20Transit

Tokyo → Hiroshima Overnight Bus

Free morning in Tokyo. Depart around evening/night by highway bus and sleep on the way.

This saves one hotel night and keeps the budget under control.

Day 6
Sep 21Hiroshima

Peace Memorial Park + Mazda Museum

Morning: Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park and Atomic Bomb Dome. Afternoon: Mazda Museum and factory tour.

Mazda Museum is free but reservation-only. Book this date early.

Day 7
Sep 22Miyajima

Miyajima Island → Osaka

Floating torii gate, Itsukushima Shrine, deer, waterfront walk. Evening bus to Osaka.

Do Miyajima early so the evening transfer does not feel rushed.

Day 8
Sep 23Osaka

Osaka Main Day

Dotonbori, Namba, Shinsaibashi, Osaka Castle, street food and night lights.

Stay around Namba if possible. It makes the night easier.

Day 9
Sep 24Kyoto

Kyoto Day Trip

Fushimi Inari early, Gion streets, Nishiki Market, then return to Osaka by train.

Start early. Kyoto crowds get annoying fast.

Day 10
Sep 25Transit

Osaka → Tokyo Overnight Bus

Free day in Osaka, then overnight bus back to Tokyo.

If budget allows, swap this for Shinkansen to make the trip more comfortable.

Day 11
Sep 26Tokyo

Last Tokyo Day

Nakano Broadway, Ghibli merch, shopping, anime cafés, TeamLab or Odaiba if wanted.

Keep this as flexible shopping and final catch-up day.

Day 12
Sep 27Home

Tokyo → Calgary

Fly home from Narita. Keep luggage simple and leave early for airport.

No big plan this day. Airport, food, flight.
